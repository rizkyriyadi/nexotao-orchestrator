# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or appearance.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, assume good intent while addressing concrete impact, welcome questions, accept constructive feedback, and keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and the work. Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the same repository private-advisory channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), with “Code of Conduct” in the summary. Project maintainers are responsible for fair, proportionate action and for protecting reporter privacy. They may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

This compact policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html>.
